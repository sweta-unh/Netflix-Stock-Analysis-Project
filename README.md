# Netflix-Stock-Analysis-Project

Introducing Netflix Stock Analysis Project, a comprehensive exploration of historical stock data for Netflix. This project aims to provide insights into stock performance, trends, and key financial metrics over time through detailed visualizations and statistical analysis.
---

## üìù Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Netflix's historical stock data to identify trends and patterns. The analysis includes:

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Time series analysis of stock prices
- Visualizations of key metrics such as opening and closing prices, volume, and adjusted close prices
- Identification of significant trends and anomalies in the stock data

## üìà Insights from the Analysis

- Long-term Growth and Volatility: Netflix's stock has shown significant growth over the years, with noticeable periods of sharp increases and decreases. These fluctuations often align with broader market trends, company performance, or industry changes.

- Significant Price Movements: The analysis highlights key times when the stock price changed dramatically, often tied to events like earnings reports, market announcements, or strategic business decisions. These moments show how external factors can impact stock performance.

- Volume Fluctuations: Examining trading volume reveals patterns in market behavior, with notable spikes often occurring before or after major price changes. This suggests strong investor interest and market reactions during these periods.

- Seasonal Patterns: The data shows trends in trading activity that may be seasonal, linked to quarterly earnings, holiday seasons, or annual financial reviews, indicating predictable cycles in market behavior.
